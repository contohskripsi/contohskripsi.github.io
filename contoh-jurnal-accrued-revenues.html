Net Income = Revenue - Expenses

Net Income = 330,000 - 165,000

Net Income = 165,000

Tambahan:

How much equity in the beginning and at the end of year?

H = U + M

210,000 = 120,000 + M

M = 90,000 (Modal awal tahun)

Modal akhir = Modal awal + Net Income - Dividend

Modal akhir = 90,000 + 165,000 - 60,000
